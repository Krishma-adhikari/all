Kanha

Kanha, also known as Lord Krishna, is the symbol of joy, wisdom, and compassion. His flute enchants not only Radha but the entire universe.

Some name of Kanha are listed below:

  1. Shree Krishna.
  2. Vasudev
  3. Kanaiya
  4. Hari
  5. Shyama
  6. Gopal
  7. Govinda
  8. Girdhari
  9. Radha ballava
If we chant Om krishnaya basudevaye haraye parmatnane pranataha kleshanashaya govindaya namo namah. Then we can achieve anything we want.I want to surrender myself to radhe shyam.
